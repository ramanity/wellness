Feeling... lonely? You are not alone

64% of college students said they had felt “very lonely” from a 2017. We believe that virtal learning has further increased these feelings of loneliness, and have compiled some strategies for you.

How to Manage Feelings of Loneliness During COVID

Try these tips

  • Keep to a Schedule
  • Stay Informed (while also limiting media consumption to an extent)
  • Stay Active
  • Do Something Meaningful
  • Connect with Others
  • Find Sources of Comfort
  • Create Something
  • Find Healthy Distractions (watching tv, listening to music, read)
  • Practice self compassion
  • Make plans for the future

Key Takeaways

Focus on your friendships and relationships with loved ones. Get comfortable in your own company [go to the movies alone, keep a gratitude journal, etc.]. Reach out to others and practice small talk (that is friendly + inviting). Pursue a hobby.
