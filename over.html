Feeling overwhelmed? Take some time out for yourself

Overwhelmed with examination stress?

  • Take a balanced approach to exams
  • Eating healthy certainly helps in late night study sessions
  • Engage in group study

Overwhelmed by the ongoing pandemic?

  • Limit screen time
  • Practice meditation
  • Learn a new skill
  • Schedule / plan out your time

Defining Overwhelm: "Having too many options or not enough time, and feeling stressed as a result."
