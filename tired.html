Feeling tired?

Take a nap!

Symptoms when you're tired:

  • Headache
  • Dizziness
  • Sore muscles
  • Slow reflexes + responses
  • Moodiness (ie: irritability)
  • Loss of appetite
  • Poor concentration

Try these tips

  • Maintain a sleep schedule
  • Exercise - get moving!
  • Identify the core root of your stress- reducing stress boosts your energy
  • Take a social media detox
  • Adopt mindful eating habits- eat healthy, balanced meals

"According to psychotherapist Lucy Beresford, humans are 'hard-wired for growth and stimulation'. This means that without a purpose for each day, we tend to feel 'defeated, lethargic or even depressed' she told HuffPost UK."

" To summarise, each day should provide a balance of work and play, including exercise, social interaction, intellectual stimulus and sleep."
