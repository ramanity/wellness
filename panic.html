Feeling panicky? Take a deep breath

Think you're having a panic/anxiety attack? Check these symptoms:

  • sweating
  • pounding heart rate
  • chills
  • trembling/shaking
  • hyperventilation (over breathing)
  • fer of loss of control
  • shortness of breath/tightness in your throat

What can you do about it?

  • Practice breathing: Practice guided meditation, yoga, light exercise
  • Relax your muscles
  • H.A.L.T your attack (check in with yourself: are you hungry/ angry/tired/lonely)
  • Keep a journal to better regulate your emotions
